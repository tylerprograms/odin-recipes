Reuben

Description

This Reuben is a classic deli sandwich stacked with bold, savory flavor in every bite. It’s made with warm, tender corned beef and pastrami layered with tangy sauerkraut, melted Swiss cheese, and creamy Russian dressing, all grilled between slices of buttery rye bread. The outside gets perfectly crisp while the inside stays melty and rich, giving you that ideal balance of crunch, saltiness, and tang. It’s a hearty, satisfying sandwich that’s simple, iconic, and hard to beat.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Lewis® hearty rye bread
  • 30g Russian dressing
  • 28g Amish Farms® baby swiss cheese (thinly sliced)
  • 84g Kretschmar® pastrami
  • 84g Eckrich® corned beef
  • 60g sauerkraut

Steps

  1. Take two slices of bread, add RUSSIAN DRESSING onto both, and spread out evenly.
  2. Add 1 slice of baby swiss to each slice of bread.
  3. Add the corned beef and pastrami to one of the slices of bread and close the sandwich with the other slice.
  4. Lay sandwich on parchment paper, fold closed, and heat in the microwave for 1 minute.
  5. Take sandwich out of parchment paper and add it to a preheated pan on medium heat until bottom side is browned.
  6. Once bottom side is browned, flip and repeat.
  7. Add sauerkraut to the sandwich, cut in half, and it’s time to eat.
